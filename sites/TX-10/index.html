About Us

We are a team of students passionate about technology, productivity, and problem-solving. Our interests range from athletics like pickleball and tennis to music and creative exploration.

Through FocusAI, we wanted to build something that directly impacts students like us—helping us stay focused in a world full of distractions.

FocusAI

FocusAI is a productivity application designed to help students overcome digital distractions and build better focus habits.

It integrates a Chrome extension that blocks distracting websites with an intuitive task management system, allowing users to stay accountable and organized.

Rather than just restricting access, FocusAI encourages intentional productivity—helping students develop discipline and improve their workflow.

Built with: React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
